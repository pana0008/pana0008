Who am I?

My name is Gabriela and I have recently began my first year at HZ University of Applied Sciences as an ICT student.

The HBO-ICT programme and the ICT field in general suits me because it combines my passion for technology and creativity. I love problem-solving, coding, and learning new tools, which are essential skills in game development. I'm drawn to the collaborative nature of ICT, where I can work with diverse teams to bring innovative ideas to life.
